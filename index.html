Diploma: How to get a Diploma from Cloud Campus

  • Learner will be eligible for Diploma if S/He completes the 3 Courses(each course should be 120 hr) in time span of 1 Year.
  • The period of 1 years will be calculated from Date of Issuance of ERA Login of 1st Course till the date of Final Exam Attempt of 3rd Course.
  • Learner can complete Diploma with combination of any 3 Courses.
  • Learner can take admission for all the 3 courses at a time or as per his/her convenience.
  • Learner can do these courses one after another or simultaneously.
  • Learner has to successfully complete 3 Courses to become eligible for Diploma within schedule.
  • Cloud Campus will provide the Diploma Certificate to eligible Learner.
  • Learner will also get the individual Course Certificates along with Diploma Certificate.

Admission Process & How to get Access :

  • Purchase Confirmation: After purchasing a course, check your email for a confirmation message from the provider, usually containing a link to access the course.
  • Accessing the Course:
    • Locate Email: Look for an email from us courses@cloudcampus.com.
    • Follow Link: Click on the link provided in the email to access your course.
    • Create/Log In: If prompted, create an account or log in to your existing account on the platform.
  • Troubleshooting:
    • No Confirmation Email?: If you didn't receive the confirmation email, contact the course creator or support team to resend the link.
    • Email Issues: Ensure that your email address was entered correctly during purchase; if not, request an update from support.
  • Navigating to your Course:
    • Once logged in, find your purchased courses section, often labeled as "My Courses" or "Purchased Courses" on the platform.
  • Supported Contacts: If you encounter issues, reach out to customer support with details of your purchase for assistance.
